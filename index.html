Email + OTP system

Validate signup verification automatically.

Signup bots use controlled test inboxes such as bot001@test.internal. OTP codes are captured, extracted, scoped to the matching bot session, injected into the flow, and expired after completion.

  1. Bot signs up with approved test data.
  2. Backend sends email with OTP.
  3. Test inbox captures the message.
  4. OTP is extracted and entered.
  5. Account creation is confirmed.
